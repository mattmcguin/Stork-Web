About Stork

Optimizing convenience.

Join Us

Mission Statement

We aim to help college students accomplish tasks quickly and effectively, as well as provide a source of revenue at their convenience.

Our Solution

Stork: A crowdsourced on-demand errand running community for college kids like you. We understand that in your busy life as a student, you might need help accomplishing day-to-day tasks, as well as a little extra cash.
Place requests to...

  • pick up your dinner when you're in the middle of an all-nighter
  • grab toothpaste when you're strapped for time and can't run to the store
  • print your paper when you remember a hard-copy is due next class

    • Fellow storks can then carry out your requests in exchange for a small fee, and you can do the same to earn an extra bit of cash.

    Our onboarding process is the dual verification of a .edu address and using a Facebook social login. With this dual verification, you can start making requests, and fulfilling them, the same day you download the app. This provides revenue stream at your convenience and help when you need it.

    Encourage your friends to become Storks and grow the community! When mustering grows, everyone benefits: it's more likely that you can give and fulfill requests without delay.

    Value Proposition

    Stork boasts many improvements over other delivery services like OrderUp or Grubhub, the most prevalent being that we are not hindered by partnerships. No company onboarding means it's convenient to be a Stork; Storks can enter an establishment as a patron and simply place an order or pick up an item alongside their own. Imagine picking up an extra burrito for dinner and dropping it off on your way home. You've gotten your meal for half price with little work, helping two Storks with one stone.

    As the Stork population grows, Storks ready to lend a hand will outnumber the amount of requests placed, meaning our wait times will be considerably shorter than those of traditional delivery services.

    There is no inherent delivery minimum- the request fee only needs to be enough of an incentive for a Stork to take it.

    By using our mobile application, we can revolutionize the way students at universities accomplish a variety of tasks and errands. Join us today!
